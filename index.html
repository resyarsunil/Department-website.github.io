Courses We Offer

The School of Data Analytics at Mahatma Gandhi University offers specialized programs designed to equip students with cutting-edge skills in data science, analytics, and computational techniques.

BSc Data Science

A four-year program aimed at students who have completed higher secondary education, offering a seamless academic path in data science.

MSc Data Science and Analytics

A two-year postgraduate program that blends core concepts of data science, machine learning, and big data analytics with hands-on training in industry-standard tools.
