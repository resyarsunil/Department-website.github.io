Who We Are

The School of Data Science and Analytics (SDA) at Mahatma Gandhi University, established in 2020, is dedicated to pioneering data-driven education, research, and innovation.

Our Vision

To emerge as a global center of excellence in data science and analytics, empowering students to drive innovation across industries.

Our Mission

To deliver transformative education, interdisciplinary research, and industry-aligned training, enriching student growth and societal impact.

Core Values

Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, Sustainability, and Social Responsibility shape our academic and research culture.
